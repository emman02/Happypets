Cage

Pet crates are designed to replicate a pet's natural den and as such can provide them with a place of refuge at home or when traveling to new surroundings. Crate training accustoms the dog/cat with the crate. The most common reasons for using a pet crate are for toilet training a new puppy or kitten, taking a pet on short trips inside the car, displaying them at a pet show, or giving a pet a place to go when visitors come to the house.
